About my university

Hanoi school of Business and Management is one of 12 university-level member units in the model of Vietnam National University, Hanoi. The school was established on July 13, 1995 by the decision of the late professor, academician Nguyen Van Dao, a beloved teacher and also the first director of VNU. This is considered as a pioneering model of graduate education in Vietnam.
